## Description


## Ingredients
1Oz Funky White Rum (Wray & Nephew)
1 oz green Chartreuse
1 oz Maraschino liqueur 
1 oz lime juice
## Method

Shake, double strain into a chilled Nick and Nora or coupe, express a lime peel and drop it in.
