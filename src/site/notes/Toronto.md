## Description

Usually uses Rye, but bourbon would sub fine.
## Ingredients


50ml Rye 
10ml Fernet 
10ml simple syrup (or even better with maple) 
1 dash Ango bitters 

## Method

Stir,
strain, 
nick and Nora, 
orange twist
