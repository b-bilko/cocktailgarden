## Description


## Ingredients

45ml Tequila Blanco
22.5ml Orange Liqueur (curacao)
7.5ml Mezcal (salmiana)
22.5ml Maple Syrup
30ml Lime Juice

## Method
Salt Rim
Shake with ice and fine strain into chilled coupe.
