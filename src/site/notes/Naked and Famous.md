## Description
A modern classic created by Joaquín Simó of Death & Co.

## Ingredients

• ¾ oz Green Chartreuse 
• ¾ oz mezcal 
• ¾ oz Aperol 
• ¾ oz fresh lime juice
## Method

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled coupe glass.
