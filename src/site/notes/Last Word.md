## Description


## Ingredients

1 oz gin
1 oz green Chartreuse
1 oz Maraschino liqueur
1 oz lime juice

## Method

Add ingredients to cocktail shaker and shake with ice. Strain into coupe or martini glass. Maraschino cherry optional.
