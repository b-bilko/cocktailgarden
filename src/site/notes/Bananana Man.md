## Description


## Ingredients
1 oz white rum 
0.5 oz Jamaican rum 
0.75 oz creme de banana 
0.5 oz lemon juice 
0.75 oz [[Black Pepper Syrup\|Black Pepper Syrup]]
1 whole egg

## Method

Dry shake to emulsify. Wet shake to chill. Double strain into coupe. Garnish with fresh cracked black pepper.
