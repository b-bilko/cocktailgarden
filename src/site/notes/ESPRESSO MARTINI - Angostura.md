## Description


## Ingredients

1 ½ oz Tito’s Vodka 
½ oz Coffee liqueur 
1 oz Brewed espresso coffee 
½ oz Simple syrup (1-1) 
6 dashes ANGOSTURA® cocoa bitters

## Method

Build in shaker, shake serve in martini glass, garnich with Coffee beans
