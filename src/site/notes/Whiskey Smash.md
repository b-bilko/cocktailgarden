## Description


## Ingredients

.75 oz. or 22 ml. Simple Syrup 
2 oz. or 60 ml. Bourbon 
1 Lemon
Mint

## Method

Build in you shaker 
Cut a lemon into wedges 
Add 4 lemon wedges 
.75 oz. or 22 ml. Simple Syrup 
2 oz. or 60 ml. Bourbon 
Add mint to taste 
Add ice and shake 
Open pour into glass
