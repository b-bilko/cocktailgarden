## Description


## Ingredients

.75 oz. or 22 ml. Lime Juice
.5 oz. or 15 ml. Grapefruit Juice
.5 oz. or 15 ml. Maraschino Liqueur
2 oz. or 60 ml. Rum 

## Method

Build in a shaker 
Add ice and shake 
Strain into glass
Garnish with a lime wheel
